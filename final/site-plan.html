Overview

Purpose

This travel journal is a fun way to share my favorite trips, photos, and travel tips from places I’ve visited.

Audience

It’s for anyone who loves exploring new spots or just wants some ideas for their next adventure.

Dynamic elements

On the site, JavaScript interacts with the DOM in a few fun ways. When you click the “Show Random Travel Tip” button, it picks a random tip from an array and updates a text box on the page. The trip cards are also generated dynamically using JavaScript. They’re pulled from an array of trip objects and added to the page with images, descriptions, and a “Favorite” button. Clicking that button updates the trip’s favorite status and adds a little heart icon to show it’s been marked. There’s also a filter section with checkboxes that lets you sort trips by region; selecting or unselecting a region updates what cards are shown, all done through DOM manipulation and event listeners.
